Exosphere is split into several specialized repositories. As a developer, you will primarily interact with:

-   **`crush`**: The core runtime and VM.
-   **`crush-capsules`**: The library of guest runtimes and system tools.
-   **`crush-sdk`**: The tools for building your own capsules.
-   **`crushed-book`**: (You are here) The unified manual and specifications.
